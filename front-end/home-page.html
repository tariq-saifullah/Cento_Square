LOGIC BEHIND THE NAME

The name CentoSquare has a deeply motivating logic that plays a monumental role in our growth and keeping our teams inspired. The name constitutes two distinct parts Cento and Square. Cento is an Italian word for Hundred, we chose Cento for we believe in catering 100% satisfaction & relief to our clients. As for square, we always had the idea of growing into an extended community of inventive and innovative people. Square represents the idea of growing exponentially not just in size but in expertise as well.
