OUR WORKING PROCESS

Our working process is pretty much flexible and varies from task to task. However, we follow some standards for all our services.

Idea Interpretation

For any task it is crucial to get a clear picture of the idea. The initial phase for any project, whether it be development, digitizing, marketing, branding or SEO, is understanding the business requirements of our clients, what actually their business is and what they want. We communicate through every possible channel with our clients to perceive their ideas as per their requirements. However, we also ask for documented requirements from our clients as to avoid inconvenience for both the parties. We are always open to communication, negotiation, and modifications regarding your projects.

Optimum Solution

After idea interpretation the second phase is to design an optimum solution to develop your idea or to solve your problem. There could be multiple ways to solve a problem, but it is important to come up something optimal. An optimum solution is best not only in terms of final outcomes but also cost and time efficient.

Milestones & Feedback

We usually break project tasks into milestones. On completion of each milestone, we share the project progress with the client in order to get their feedback. Your feedback means a lot to us. In software development projects, we incorporate agile process model as this model proposes incremental and iterative approach to software design.

Final Product

Whatever your project is, we make sure to test and analyze every aspect before releasing/delivering it to you. We verify the final product/outcome as per your stated requirements.
